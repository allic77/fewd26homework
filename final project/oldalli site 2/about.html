10+ years of experience advancing brands, regional & national programs, events &marketing campaigns — for businesses, corporations & government agencies

I’m Allison. A Marylander born and bred, who thinks any crab that’s not blue crab, isn’t crab at all. I did jump the Potomac for college as I became and still am a proud Hokie, who discovered the power of web design and photography in Blacksburg, along with college football. I’ve been a creative my whole life, though in my younger years this was writing more than graphics, however combining the two is still a dream of mine as I have a screenplay and children’s book I’d love to someday have produced/publish.

Visual communication and design can be as important as the written message itself. My job today is to combine the written and visual messages in ways that make sense and help businesses and government agencies bring their message to the marketplace with sound visual direction.

I’m passionate about what I believe in, have a real hunger for learning and have the drive and dedication to create great work. My graphic and web design management experience includes developing creative strategies, implementing strategic vision objectives, and creating websites, infographics, and social media messages. My professional expertise lies in print and digital projects, including marketing campaigns with logos, letterhead, banners, promotional items, to developing websites that include custom interactive elements that enhance the aesthetics of a site. While a lot of my experience is in print, I am now bringing my print design skills to the web by shifting focus to front-end web development and web design.

In my free time, I work as a freelance designer. When I’m not designing, I can be found hiking, trying out new restaurants or catching the latest flick.

Please drop me a line to say hello or if you are interested in more information on freelance. Thank you for visiting!
